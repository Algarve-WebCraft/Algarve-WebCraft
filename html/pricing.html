Affordable Packages for Any Business

We offer two simple options for your small business website:

The first is a one-time payment where the website is yours to keep. We can either host it for you (for a small ongoing fee), or hand over the full files so you can manage hosting yourself and use the site however you like.

The second option is our monthly plan which comes with a 6-month minimum contract and includes hosting, ongoing support and regular updates, all bundled together. One of the best parts? The price you start with is the price you will always pay, for as long as you stay on the plan. Even when our prices go up in the future, your price will never change.

Please note: if you decide to cancel the monthly plan, the website files are not included (as everyone would just cancel after the six months, making it pointless to offer in the first place). If you choose to rejoin later, it would be at the current pricing at that time.
